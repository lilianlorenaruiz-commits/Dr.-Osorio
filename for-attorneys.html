A Reliable Psychological Evaluation Practice for Legal and Immigration Cases

At Dr. Claudia Osorio Psychological Services, we provide specialized immigration and forensic psychological evaluations for attorneys and law firms across the United States.

Our reports are objective, ethical, and professionally structured to support legal cases, court documentation, immigration proceedings, and forensic matters.

Our team holds active licenses in multiple states and participates in PSYPACT, allowing us to provide telehealth evaluations across a broad U.S. footprint and expand access to our services regardless of location. Services are offered in English and Spanish, with additional language support available to help clients communicate in the language they feel most comfortable using.

Our Services

Evaluations Available

All evaluations are designed to provide objective, well-documented findings that may be used in legal, court, or immigration proceedings, depending on the nature of the case.

Immigration Psychological Evaluations

Our immigration psychological evaluations support a wide range of immigration matters by documenting the emotional and psychological impact of a client's circumstances in a clinically grounded and professionally structured report.

These may include:

  • Asylum and hardship waiver evaluations
  • VAWA, U-Visa, and T-Visa cases
  • N-648 evaluations
  • Immigration court-related competency or mitigation matters

Each evaluation is conducted with cultural sensitivity and trauma-aware methodology, providing attorneys with clear, relevant, and well-supported documentation for the case file.

Forensic Evaluations

Our forensic psychological evaluations address psycho-legal issues in criminal, civil, and family court matters, providing objective clinical analysis within a legal framework.

These may include:

  • Risk and psychosexual assessments
  • Competency and responsibility evaluations
  • Presentence and mitigation evaluations
  • Domestic violence and other forensic-related assessments

These evaluations help legal professionals access structured psychological findings that support legal understanding and case preparation.

Learn More

Psychological Evaluations

Our psychological evaluations may be necessary in legal contexts when a case requires a structured understanding of a client's emotional, cognitive, behavioral, or daily functioning.

These evaluations can help attorneys:

  • Clarify psychological challenges affecting the client's functioning
  • Document cognitive, emotional, or behavioral patterns relevant to the case
  • Provide broader psychological context for legal strategy, case analysis, or immigration matters

For attorneys, this type of evaluation is valuable when a case requires more than symptom description and calls for a nuanced psychological assessment with professionally documented findings.
