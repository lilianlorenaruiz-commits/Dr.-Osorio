Practice

Practice Overview

Dr. Claudia Osorio Psychological Services provides evidence-based psychological evaluations across three core areas: immigration psychological evaluations supporting asylum, VAWA, hardship waivers, U-Visa, T-Visa, and N-648 cases; forensic psychological evaluations for criminal, civil, and family court matters; and general psychological evaluations addressing emotional, cognitive, and behavioral functioning.

The practice serves clients in person from offices in Worcester (Massachusetts), East Hartford (Connecticut), and Palm Bay (Florida), and via secure telehealth across the 44 jurisdictions of the Psychology Interjurisdictional Compact (PSYPACT).

Credentials

Licensure and Authorization

State Licensure

Dr. Osorio holds active psychology licenses in three states:

  • Massachusetts Board of Registration of Psychologists
  • Connecticut Department of Public Health, Psychology
  • Florida Department of Health, Psychology

PSYPACT Authorization

Authorized by the Psychology Interjurisdictional Compact to provide telehealth psychological services across all 44 participating jurisdictions, including the District of Columbia and Northern Mariana Islands.

Service languages:

  • English
  • Spanish

Credential details, including license numbers and PSYPACT directory references, are available upon request for verification by attorneys, courts, and credentialing organizations.
