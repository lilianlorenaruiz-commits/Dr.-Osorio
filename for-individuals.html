For Individuals & Families

Psychological Evaluations for Individuals and Families

At Dr. Claudia Osorio Psychological Services, we provide specialized psychological evaluations and clinical psychological assessments for individuals and families. Whether you need support for an immigration matter, a psychological evaluation, or a forensic-related concern, our services are designed to provide clarity, professional documentation, and a structured understanding of your situation.

Each evaluation is tailored to assess emotional, cognitive, behavioral, and psychological functioning and help you move forward with confidence.

Evaluations Available

Find the Right Evaluation for Your Situation

Each evaluation is designed to address a specific need. Review the options below to identify which type of evaluation fits your situation.

Immigration Psychological Evaluations

Our immigration psychological evaluations support individuals undergoing immigration proceedings by providing psychological reports that document the emotional impact of their circumstances and help strengthen their cases. These evaluations may include asylum and CAT evaluations, VAWA and domestic violence evaluations, T-Visa and U-Visa evaluations, hardship waiver evaluations, N-648 evaluations, and selected immigration court-related forensic assessments. Each evaluation is conducted with cultural sensitivity to accurately reflect the client's unique experience.

Learn More

Forensic Evaluations

Our forensic evaluations are designed to address psycho-legal issues in criminal, civil, and family court cases when a formal psychological assessment is needed. These evaluations may include risk and psychosexual assessments, domestic violence evaluations, parental fitness evaluations, and other court-related assessment needs. Each evaluation is designed to provide objective, professionally documented findings that support legal understanding and decision-making.

Learn More

Psychological Evaluations

Our psychological evaluations provide a comprehensive view of the factors affecting behavior, cognitive abilities, social and emotional functioning, and daily life. These assessments help clients better understand their strengths and challenges and offer personalized recommendations for well-being. Comprehensive assessments are designed to identify and understand cognitive, emotional, and psychological challenges, including trauma, anxiety, and depression. Through a collaborative and nuanced evaluation process, each assessment is tailored to the client's unique emotional and psychological needs.
